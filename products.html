Mid-Face Volumisation & Contouring

The formulation supports both linear threading and bolus injection techniques, making it the versatile workhorse for practices treating a broad range of patient profiles and treatment plans.

AestheFill Deep

Structural Correction & Contouring

AestheFill Deep is formulated for advanced practitioners who work at the deepest anatomical levels. Its high-density, highly cross-linked matrix provides the mechanical support required for structural correction — jawline definition, chin projection, and deep malar enhancement — with results that stand the test of time.

Suitable for both supraperiosteal and deep dermal placement. Compatible with cannula techniques. Recommended for practitioners with advanced anatomical training and demonstrable experience in deep-plane injection.

Advanced Practitioners: This product is recommended for clinicians with advanced anatomical training in deep-plane facial injection. Clinical assessment of patient suitability is the practitioner's responsibility.
